FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

What file formats do you accept?

We accept all major CAD formats including STEP (.STEP/.STP), IGES (.IGES/.IGS), STL, SolidWorks (.SLDPRT), Parasolid (.X_T), ACIS (.SAT), AutoCAD (.DWG/.DXF), and PDF drawings. You can also send a ZIP archive with multiple files. If you have a different format, contact us.

What's your typical lead time?

Standard lead time is 7–10 business days. Expedited service is available at 10–15 days, and extended timelines (15–25 days) offer the most competitive pricing. Choose "Flexible" on the quote form for the best price.

Do you offer design assistance?

Yes! Our engineering team provides free DFM (Design for Manufacturability) feedback with every quote to help optimize your design.

What certifications do you hold?

We are ISO9001 and IATF16949 certified. We can provide all necessary quality documentation.

Is there a minimum order quantity?

No minimum order required. We handle everything from single prototypes to production runs of 100,000+ parts.

Do you offer finishing services?

Yes, we offer anodizing, powder coating, plating, passivation, bead blasting, and more. All done in-house for quality control.
