Process Control

Quality Control Workflow

Our comprehensive quality control process ensures every part meets specifications from raw material to final delivery.

1. Incoming Material Inspection

All raw materials undergo spectroscopic analysis and dimensional verification. Material certificates are verified and archived for traceability.

2. First Article Inspection (FAI)

Complete dimensional verification of first production part using CMM. All critical dimensions documented per AS9102 or customer requirements.

3. In-Process Inspection

Operators perform regular dimensional checks using calibrated gauges. SPC charts monitor critical dimensions in real-time to detect trends.

4. Final Quality Control

100% visual inspection for cosmetic defects. Statistical sampling for dimensional verification per AQL standards. Surface finish measurement.

5. Documentation & Shipment

Complete inspection reports, CoC, and material certifications prepared. Parts carefully packaged with ESD protection where required.
