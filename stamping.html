Metal Stamping Services

High-speed precision stamping for cost-effective mass production. From progressive die stamping to deep drawing, we deliver consistent quality at scale with tight tolerances.

Request Quote Consult Engineering Team
High Efficiency

Why Choose Metal Stamping?

Metal stamping offers unmatched speed and cost-efficiency for high-volume production of precision parts.

High-Speed Production

Produce thousands of identical parts per hour with automated progressive die systems. Ideal for large volume requirements.

Cost Effective

Low per-part cost at volume due to minimal material waste and high automation. Excellent ROI for production runs.

Tight Tolerances

Achieve tolerances as tight as ±0.05mm with precision tooling. Consistent dimensional accuracy across millions of parts.

Part Repeatability

Once tooling is qualified, every part is virtually identical. Perfect for interchangeable components and assemblies.

Processes

Stamping Methods

We offer various stamping processes to meet your specific part geometry and production volume requirements.

Progressive Die Stamping

Multiple operations performed sequentially as material advances through die stations. Most efficient for high-volume complex parts.

  • High-speed automated production
  • Complex geometries in single setup
  • Ideal for 10,000+ parts

Deep Drawing

Form sheet metal into deep, cup-shaped or box-shaped components. Creates seamless enclosures and containers.

  • Seamless enclosed shapes
  • Depth-to-diameter ratios up to 2:1
  • Housings, cups, enclosures

Blanking & Piercing

Cut flat parts from sheet metal (blanking) or create holes and cutouts (piercing) with clean edges and tight tolerances.

  • Clean, burr-free edges
  • Precise hole positioning
  • Flat parts and washers

Bending & Forming

Create angles, channels, and complex 3D shapes by plastically deforming sheet metal around a die radius.

  • V-bends, U-bends, Z-bends
  • Tight bend radii control
  • Brackets, clips, channels
