Medical Parts Gallery

Surgical instruments, implants, and medical device components

Surgical Instrument Bodies

Forceps, retractors, and trocar bodies in 316L stainless and titanium Grade 23. Passivated and electropolished to ISO 13485 surface standards.

Orthopedic Implants

Bone screws, plates, and intramedullary nails in Ti-6Al-4V ELI. Surface roughness Ra ≤ 0.4μm with biocompatibility certification.

Endoscopy Components

Diagnostic Equipment Parts
