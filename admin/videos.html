Video Guidelines

Recommended Video Specifications

Hero Video MP4/WebM, 1920x1080, Under 10MB, No audio required
Company Video MP4/WebM, 1920x1080, Under 50MB, Audio supported
Poster Image JPG/PNG, 16:9 ratio, At least 600x400px

Supported Video Sources

  • Local files in website directory (e.g., hero-video.mp4)
  • Direct video URLs (MP4 or WebM)
  • CDN hosted videos
Note: Large video files may affect page loading speed. Consider using compressed videos or CDN hosting for better performance.
