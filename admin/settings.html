Logo Usage Guide
  • Main Logo: Displayed in the top navigation bar and footer. Use your full company logo with text.
  • Favicon: Displayed in browser tabs, bookmarks, and mobile home screens. Use a simple icon version without text.
  • Format: PNG with transparent background is recommended for best results.
  • Note: After saving, refresh the browser to see changes (Ctrl+F5 or Cmd+Shift+R).
