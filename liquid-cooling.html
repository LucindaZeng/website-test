High Performance

Skived Fin Heat Sinks

Ultra-thin copper fins for maximum thermal conductivity and heat dissipation in high-power applications.

Precision Skiving Technology

Our skived fin heat sinks are manufactured using advanced skiving technology that creates ultra-thin fins directly from solid copper blocks. This one-piece construction eliminates thermal interface resistance between fins and base, achieving superior thermal performance.

  • Fin thickness: 0.3mm - 1.0mm
  • Fin height: Up to 60mm
  • Fin pitch: 1.0mm - 3.0mm
  • Materials: C1100 pure copper, C1020 oxygen-free copper

Applications

CPU/GPU Cooling IGBT Modules Power Electronics LED Lighting 5G Base Stations Data Centers

Cold Forging

Forged Pin Fin Heat Sinks

High-density pin fin heat sinks manufactured through precision cold forging for omnidirectional airflow.

Round Pin Fins

Cylindrical pins for uniform omnidirectional airflow. Ideal for natural convection and forced air cooling.

  • Pin diameter: 2mm - 8mm
  • Pin height: Up to 50mm
  • Aluminum & copper

Square Pin Fins

Rectangular pins for increased surface area and structural rigidity. Excellent for high-power applications.

  • Pin size: 3mm - 10mm
  • High pin density
  • Enhanced heat transfer

Radial Pin Fins

Pins arranged in radial pattern for cylindrical heat sources. Perfect for LED and spotlight cooling.

  • 360° heat dissipation
  • Custom configurations
  • Aesthetic design
