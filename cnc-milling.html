Our Capabilities

Advanced Milling Technologies

State-of-the-art equipment and expert programming deliver exceptional results for any geometry.

3-Axis Milling

Cost-effective machining for prismatic parts, enclosures, brackets, and components with features on one side.

4-Axis Milling

Rotary axis capability for cylindrical features, radial holes, and parts requiring machining on multiple sides.

5-Axis Milling

Simultaneous 5-axis for complex aerospace and medical parts with undercuts, compound angles, and sculptured surfaces.

High-Speed Machining

40,000+ RPM spindles for superior surface finishes and efficient aluminum and plastic machining.

Hard Milling

Direct machining of hardened steels up to 65 HRC, reducing the need for EDM and grinding operations.

Large Part Machining

Work envelope up to 80" x 40" x 30" for oversized components and structural parts.
