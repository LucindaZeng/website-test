FAQ

Rapid Prototyping — Frequently Asked Questions

How fast can you deliver a CNC prototype?

CNC machined prototypes ship in as little as 3–5 business days. Because we run 300+ machines in-house, we don't queue behind production jobs — prototypes get scheduled fast. Confirmed lead time comes with every quote.

What is the minimum order for a prototype?

There is no minimum — we machine a single prototype part. The same engineering and quality process applies whether you order one piece or a thousand, so your prototype matches eventual production.

Can my prototype be made in the same material as production?

Yes, and we recommend it. Machining the prototype in the production material (e.g. aluminum 6061, titanium, PEEK) gives you accurate functional testing — real strength, weight, and finish — rather than a cosmetic mockup.

Do you provide DFM feedback on prototypes?

Yes. Every prototype quote includes design-for-manufacturability feedback within 24 hours — our engineers flag features that are costly, fragile, or hard to machine, so you can refine the design before committing to production.

Can you scale from prototype to production?

Yes — that's our core strength. Once your prototype is validated, we move to volume production on the same equipment and quality system (ISO9001, IATF16949), avoiding the re-qualification delays of switching suppliers.
