Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about our CNC machining services.

General Questions

We accept all major CAD formats including STEP (.STEP/.STP), IGES (.IGES/.IGS), STL, SolidWorks (.SLDPRT), Parasolid (.X_T), ACIS (.SAT), AutoCAD (.DWG/.DXF), PDF drawings, and ZIP archives with multiple files. If you have a different format, please contact us.
Standard lead time is 7–10 business days. Expedited service is available at 10–15 days, and extended timelines (15–25 days) offer the most competitive pricing. Complex parts may require additional time.
No minimum order required. We handle everything from single prototypes to production runs of 100,000+ parts. Our pricing is competitive at any volume.

Technical Questions

Standard tolerances are ±0.005" (0.127mm). Precision tolerances to ±0.005mm (0.0127mm) are available for critical features. Tighter tolerances may be possible depending on geometry.
We machine 50+ materials including aluminum alloys, stainless steel, steel, titanium, brass, copper, engineering plastics (Delrin, PEEK, Ultem, Nylon), and exotic alloys (Inconel, Hastelloy).
We are ISO9001 and IATF16949 certified. We provide complete quality documentation including inspection reports, material certifications, and certificates of conformance.

Ordering & Shipping

Yes, we offer anodizing (Type II & III), powder coating, plating, passivation, bead blasting, electropolishing, and more. All finishing is done in-house for quality control.
Yes, we ship worldwide. We have experience with international shipping and can handle export documentation. DHL, FedEx, and UPS options are available.
