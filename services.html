Precision Manufacturing Services

End-to-end precision manufacturing under one roof — from 5-axis CNC machining of titanium aerospace brackets to high-volume die casting and full assembly. All ISO 9001 and IATF 16949 certified.

CNC Milling

3-axis to 5-axis CNC milling on DMG MORI, Mazak, and Hermle. Tolerances to ±0.005mm on aluminum, steel, titanium, and engineering plastics.

Learn more

CNC Turning

Swiss-type and conventional CNC lathes for shafts, bushings, connectors, and precision turned parts. Live tooling for milled features.

Learn more

5-Axis Machining

Simultaneous 5-axis on DMG MORI DMU 50 and Mazak Variaxis. Impellers, turbine blades, complex aerospace and medical geometries in one setup.

Learn more

Die Casting

High-pressure die casting in aluminum (A380, ADC12) and zinc alloys. From prototype tooling to high-volume production runs.

Learn more

Investment Casting

Lost-wax investment casting in stainless steel, carbon steel, and copper alloys. Near-net shapes with excellent surface finish.

Learn more

Forging

Open-die and closed-die forging for high-strength structural components. Grain flow optimization for fatigue-critical parts.

Learn more

Sheet Metal

Laser cutting, CNC bending, welding, and finishing. Enclosures, brackets, chassis, and structural sheet metal assemblies.

Learn more

Stamping

Progressive die and compound die stamping. High-volume contacts, brackets, shields, and electrical components.

Learn more

Surface Finishing

Anodizing (Type II/III), powder coating, electroplating, passivation, polishing, sandblasting, and laser engraving — full in-house capability.

Learn more

Assembly

Sub-assembly and full product assembly with bonded, fastened, and welded joining. Functional testing before shipment.

Learn more

Production Runs

Low- to mid-volume production with consistent quality and on-time delivery. PPAP documentation available for automotive and medical.

Learn more

Precision Inspection

Zeiss and Hexagon CMM inspection, optical measurement, surface roughness verification. Full First Article Inspection reports.

Learn more
