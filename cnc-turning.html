CNC Turning Services

High-precision turned components for cylindrical parts, shafts, and complex rotational geometries. Our advanced CNC lathes with live tooling deliver exceptional quality.

Advanced CNC Turning Technology

Live Tooling

Mill, drill, and tap while the part is still in the lathe. Reduces setups and improves accuracy.

Sub-Spindle Operations

Complete back-side machining in a single setup for maximum efficiency and precision.

Bar Feed Automation

Continuous production with automatic bar feeding for high-volume orders.
