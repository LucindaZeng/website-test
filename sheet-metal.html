Sheet Metal Fabrication

Complete sheet metal solutions from prototype to production. Precision laser cutting, CNC bending, welding, and finishing for enclosures, brackets, chassis, and custom fabrications.

Versatile Manufacturing

Why Choose Sheet Metal Fabrication?

Sheet metal offers design flexibility, rapid turnaround, and cost-effective solutions for a wide range of applications.

Design Flexibility

Create complex geometries, enclosures, and structural components. Easy to modify designs and iterate during development.

Rapid Turnaround

Quick production with no tooling required for prototypes. From design to finished parts in days, not weeks.

Light & Strong

Excellent strength-to-weight ratio. Ideal for enclosures, brackets, and structural components where weight matters.

Cost Scalability

Economical for prototypes and production. Per-part cost decreases with volume while maintaining quality.

Processes

Fabrication Capabilities

Comprehensive sheet metal processing from cutting to finishing, all under one roof.

Laser Cutting

High-precision fiber laser cutting for clean edges and intricate details. Capable of cutting various materials and thicknesses.

  • Tolerance: ±0.1mm
  • Steel up to 20mm thick
  • Complex contours and cutouts

CNC Press Brake Bending

Precision CNC press brakes for accurate and repeatable bends. Multiple bend sequences in a single setup.

  • Angular accuracy: ±0.5°
  • Bending length up to 3000mm
  • Complex multi-bend parts

Welding & Joining

Multiple welding processes for strong, clean joints. Certified welders for structural and precision assemblies.

  • TIG, MIG, spot welding
  • Laser welding available
  • Hardware insertion (PEM)

CNC Punching

High-speed CNC turret punching for holes, louvers, and formed features. Cost-effective for high-volume production.

  • Standard and custom tooling
  • Forming, tapping, countersinking
  • Louvers and embossing
