Our Story

19+ Years of Precision Manufacturing Excellence

Founded in 2007, Dongguan Wanfuxin Intelligent Equipment Co., Ltd. (WFX) is a leading CNC precision machining manufacturer located in Qingxi Town, Dongguan City, Guangdong Province. We specialize in providing high-precision CNC machining services to customers worldwide across automotive, aerospace, liquid cooling, medical, and electronics industries.

Our modern self-built facility spans 20,000 square meters and houses over 300 CNC machines running 24/7 production. We serve customers across the USA, Germany, Austria, Finland, Australia, and 10 Chinese provinces including Guangdong, Zhejiang, Jiangsu, Shandong, Beijing, and more.

With ISO9001:2015 and IATF16949:2016 certifications (verified by Quality Austria), we maintain rigorous quality standards. Our in-house capabilities include 5-axis machining, CNC turning, precision grinding, and our own anodizing subsidiary for complete surface treatment control.
