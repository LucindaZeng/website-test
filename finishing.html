Electroplating

Deposit metal coatings through electrochemical process for enhanced properties

Zinc Plating

Electrochemical process that deposits a protective zinc layer on steel and iron parts, providing excellent corrosion resistance through sacrificial protection.

  • Excellent corrosion resistance
  • Cost-effective protection
  • Various finishes: clear, yellow, black
  • RoHS compliant options available
Applications: Fasteners, brackets, automotive parts, hardware

Nickel Plating

Creates a hard, lustrous coating that provides excellent wear resistance and decorative appeal. Available in bright, satin, and electroless variants.

  • High wear resistance
  • Attractive bright or satin finish
  • Good corrosion protection
  • Excellent base for other coatings
Applications: Electronics, decorative parts, industrial components

Chrome Plating

Deposits a hard chromium layer providing superior hardness, excellent wear resistance, and a brilliant decorative finish with low friction coefficient.

  • Extremely high hardness (HV 800-1000)
  • Superior wear resistance
  • Brilliant decorative appearance
  • Low coefficient of friction
Applications: Hydraulic cylinders, molds, decorative trim, tools

Copper Plating

Deposits a copper layer that provides excellent electrical conductivity, often used as a base layer for subsequent plating or for its conductive properties.

  • Excellent electrical conductivity
  • Good heat conductivity
  • Ideal undercoat for multi-layer plating
  • Improves adhesion of top coats
Applications: Electronics, EMI shielding, decorative base layer

Chemical Treatment

Surface modification through chemical reactions for protection and enhanced properties

Blackening / Black Oxide

Chemical conversion process that produces a black magnetite (Fe3O4) layer on steel and iron parts, providing rust prevention and aesthetic appeal.

  • Rust prevention for ferrous metals
  • Minimal dimensional change
  • Attractive matte black finish
  • Reduces light reflection
Applications: Tools, fasteners, machine components, firearms

Phosphating

Creates a crystalline phosphate conversion coating that enhances paint adhesion, provides moderate corrosion protection, and improves lubricity.

  • Excellent paint adhesion
  • Corrosion protection
  • Improved lubricant retention
  • Types: zinc, manganese, iron phosphate
Applications: Automotive bodies, appliances, pre-paint treatment

Passivation

Chemical treatment for stainless steel that removes free iron and enhances the natural chromium oxide layer, maximizing corrosion resistance.

  • Enhances corrosion resistance
  • Removes surface contaminants
  • No dimensional change
  • ASTM A967 / AMS2700 compliant
Applications: Medical devices, food equipment, aerospace parts

Electroless Nickel Plating

Autocatalytic chemical process that deposits uniform nickel-phosphorus alloy without external electrical current, ideal for complex geometries.

  • Uniform thickness on complex shapes
  • Excellent corrosion resistance
  • No edge buildup
  • Can plate non-conductive substrates
Applications: Valves, pumps, electronics, oil & gas equipment

Anodizing

Electrochemical process primarily for aluminum that creates a durable oxide layer

Type II Anodizing (Decorative)

Standard sulfuric acid anodizing that creates a porous oxide layer suitable for dyeing. Provides good corrosion resistance and aesthetic appeal.

  • Thickness: 8-25μm
  • Wide range of colors available
  • Good corrosion resistance
  • Moderate wear resistance
Applications: Consumer electronics, architectural, automotive trim

Type III Anodizing (Hard Coat)

Hard anodizing process that produces a much thicker, harder, and more wear-resistant oxide layer for demanding applications.

  • Thickness: 25-100μm
  • Superior hardness (60-70 HRC equivalent)
  • Excellent wear resistance
  • Natural gray-black color
Applications: Military, aerospace, hydraulic components, firearms

Coating & Painting

Apply protective and decorative coatings for enhanced appearance and protection

Spray Painting

Liquid paint application using spray guns to atomize and deposit paint onto workpiece surfaces. Offers unlimited color options and various finish effects from high gloss to matte.

Paint Types:
Solid Color Paint
Metallic Paint
Pearlescent Paint
UV Cured Paint
Rubber/Soft Touch
PU Paint
  • Flexible colors: Any custom color matching (RAL, Pantone)
  • Multiple effects: Gloss, matte, semi-matte, gradient, textured
  • Thin film: Finer surface than powder coating
  • UV paint: High hardness through UV curing
  • Soft touch: Comfortable tactile feel
Applications: Phone cases, electronics housings, automotive interior, cosmetic packaging

Powder Coating

Dry finishing process where electrostatically charged powder is applied to grounded workpieces, then heat-cured to form a durable, high-quality finish. More environmentally friendly than liquid paint with no solvent emissions.

Available Textures:
Smooth Gloss
Smooth Matte
Sand Texture
Wrinkle Texture
Hammer Tone
Metallic Effect
  • Thick coating: 60-80μm typical thickness
  • Strong adhesion: Better durability than liquid paint
  • Wide color range: RAL, Pantone, custom colors
  • Eco-friendly: No VOC emissions, overspray recyclable
  • Excellent protection: Superior corrosion and weather resistance
Applications: Cabinets, railings, furniture, appliance housings, aluminum profiles

E-Coating (Electrophoretic Coating)

Immersion coating process where workpieces are submerged in paint bath and electric current deposits paint uniformly on all surfaces. Provides excellent coverage on complex geometries and recessed areas that spray methods cannot reach.

  • Uniform coverage: Even coating in recesses and cavities
  • Excellent adhesion: Electrochemical bonding to substrate
  • Superior corrosion protection: Complete edge coverage
  • Consistent thickness: Precise film build control
  • High efficiency: 95%+ paint utilization
Applications: Automotive bodies, appliances, hardware, electrical components

Heat Treatment Surface Hardening

Thermochemical processes to increase surface hardness while maintaining core toughness

Carburizing

Diffuses carbon into the surface of low-carbon steel at high temperature, creating a hard, wear-resistant case while maintaining a tough, ductile core.

  • Case depth: 0.5-2.0mm typical
  • Surface hardness: 58-62 HRC
  • Excellent fatigue resistance
  • Maintains core toughness
Applications: Gears, shafts, bearings, camshafts

Nitriding

Diffuses nitrogen into the surface at lower temperatures than carburizing, creating extremely hard surface layer with excellent wear and fatigue resistance.

  • Surface hardness: 65-70 HRC equivalent
  • Minimal distortion (low temperature)
  • No quenching required
  • Excellent corrosion resistance
Applications: Molds, dies, crankshafts, valve components

Induction Hardening

Rapid localized heat treatment using high-frequency electromagnetic induction, followed by quenching to harden specific surface areas while minimizing distortion.

  • Precise, localized hardening
  • Fast process cycle
  • Minimal distortion
  • Selective area treatment
Applications: Shafts, gears, spindles, bearing races

Mechanical Treatment

Physical surface modification for texture, appearance, and finish enhancement

Polishing

Progressive abrasive process to achieve mirror-like surface finishes with extremely low roughness values. Includes mechanical, chemical, and electropolishing methods.

  • Mirror finish (Ra < 0.1μm possible)
  • Manual or automated processes
  • Electropolishing available
  • Removes surface imperfections
Applications: Medical devices, optics, decorative parts, molds

Sandblasting / Bead Blasting

High-pressure abrasive media treatment to create uniform matte textures, remove burrs, clean surfaces, and prepare for subsequent finishing processes.

  • Uniform matte finish
  • Effective deburring
  • Surface preparation
  • Various grit sizes available
Applications: Consumer electronics, enclosures, pre-anodizing treatment

Brushing / Hairline Finish

Creates directional linear texture (hairline pattern) on metal surfaces using abrasive belts or brushes, providing distinctive metallic appearance.

  • Distinctive linear texture
  • Hides fingerprints and scratches
  • Various grain patterns
  • Professional metallic appearance
Applications: Appliances, consumer electronics, architectural panels

Vapor Deposition Coatings

Advanced thin-film coating technologies for superior hardness and decorative effects

PVD (Physical Vapor Deposition)

Vacuum process where metal is evaporated or sputtered and deposited as a thin film onto the workpiece surface. Produces extremely thin, hard coatings with excellent wear resistance and rich decorative colors.

Common PVD Methods:
Vacuum Evaporation
Sputtering
Ion Plating
Arc Deposition
Available Colors:
Gold
Rose Gold
Black
Silver Chrome
Blue
Bronze
  • Thin film: 0.5-5μm typical thickness
  • High hardness: Up to 2500 HV
  • Excellent adhesion: Atomic-level bonding
  • Wear resistant: Extended component life
  • Low friction: Reduced wear on mating surfaces
  • Decorative: Rich color options, premium appearance
Applications: Watches, phone cases, cutting tools, molds, bathroom hardware

CVD (Chemical Vapor Deposition)

Similar to PVD but forms coating through chemical reactions of gaseous precursors on the heated workpiece surface. Produces coatings with superior adhesion strength, though requires higher process temperatures.

  • Superior adhesion: Stronger bonding than PVD
  • Conformal coating: Excellent coverage on complex shapes
  • High purity: Very pure coating composition
  • Thick films possible: Can build thicker layers
  • Process temperature: 500-1000°C typical
Applications: Cutting tools, mold coatings, semiconductor, wear parts

Marking & Printing

Add logos, text, codes, and decorative patterns to finished parts

Laser Engraving

Uses focused laser beam to ablate material surface, creating permanent high-precision marks. Non-contact process ideal for delicate parts and high-volume production.

  • High precision marking
  • Permanent, wear-resistant
  • No contact with workpiece
  • Fast cycle time for batch production
  • Variable depth control
Applications: Logos, serial numbers, QR codes, button markings, traceability

Screen Printing (Silk Screen)

Transfers ink through a fine mesh screen onto the workpiece surface. Cost-effective for large batches with vibrant colors and good ink coverage.

  • Cost-effective for high volumes
  • Vibrant colors available
  • Multi-color printing possible
  • Thick ink layer, good coverage
  • On-site capability at our facility
Applications: Control panels, button text, decorative patterns, labels

Pad Printing

Indirect offset printing using silicone pad to transfer ink from etched plate to irregular or curved surfaces not suitable for screen printing.

  • Prints on curved surfaces
  • Fine detail reproduction
  • Multi-color capability
  • Handles complex geometries
Applications: Medical devices, electronics, promotional products

Other Specialty Treatments

Additional specialized surface treatments for specific applications

Dacromet

Chrome-free, environmentally friendly anti-corrosion coating using zinc and aluminum flakes in an inorganic binder. Provides excellent corrosion protection without hexavalent chromium.

  • Chrome-free, RoHS compliant
  • Excellent corrosion resistance
  • No hydrogen embrittlement
  • Heat resistant to 300°C
  • Environmentally friendly
Applications: Automotive fasteners, brake components, wind turbine bolts

Teflon / PTFE Coating

Fluoropolymer coating providing extremely low friction coefficient, chemical resistance, and non-stick properties for demanding applications.

  • Lowest friction coefficient
  • Non-stick surface
  • Chemical resistance
  • Wide temperature range
Applications: Molds, food processing, medical, bearings

Chromate Conversion

Chemical treatment for aluminum that provides corrosion protection and improves paint adhesion. Available in clear (RoHS) and yellow/iridescent versions.

  • Corrosion protection for aluminum
  • Excellent paint adhesion
  • Conductive surface maintained
  • RoHS compliant options
Applications: Aerospace, electronics, military, pre-paint treatment
